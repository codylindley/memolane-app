Managing Your Apps

Two pages that will every developer with have to get to know are:

  • memolane.com/oauth/my_apps
  • memolane.com/oauth/authorized_apps

    • Both of these pages are essential to third party apps, the first for the sake of the developer, the other for the sake of the user subscribed to the service.

    MY APPS

    As a developer, memolane.com/oauth/my_apps will be the launch pad to integrating your service into users' Memolanes. From here you will be able to keep track of the services that you support.

    Simply click on the "Register Another Service" to access the form you will need to fill out. After registering the app, you will be provided with the key and secret you will need for authenticating your app. At any point you can go in and edit the information that you have provided.

    AUTHORIZED APPS

    As a user, memolane.com/oauth/authorized_apps will have a listing of the sites that you have granted access to push content to your Memolane.

    From here you can quickly scan to see which services you have granted access to your account. You can disable the service which will then no longer allow any future notes to be pushed. If you wish to fully wipe out any content that had been pushed by selecting delete once the service is disabled.
